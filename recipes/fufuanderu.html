FuFu and Eru

Description

FuFu and Eru is a local delicacy originating from West Africa, precisely Cameroon. The FuFu is made from Cassava and can be consumed as either garri or fermented wet Fufu. The Eru is a mixture of wild leaves and its often cooked with other leaves. it is a popular delicacy that never looses its way in an African restaurant.

Ingredients

  • Garri or FuFu powder
  • Finely sliced Eru leaves
  • Water leaf
  • Meat and or Fish
  • Palm oil
  • cray fish
  • Maggi and salt
  • Pepper

Steps

Preparing FuFu

  1. Measure the desired quantity of fufu and put in a bowl. While ist in the bowl, add water and use the hands to dissolve the fufu. continously smash the Fufu till its completely dissolved in the water.
  2. Next put the completely dissolved fufu on fire and wait for it to start boiling Once the boiling starts, start turning till the fufu is smooth.
  3. You can the remove after its smooth and serve from the pot or wrap into beautiful loaves.

Preparing Eru

  1. Clean the water leaf and place in a pot without water. This is because the leaf has its water
  2. While its boilling, wash the Eru and place in the pot
  3. Then add the fish, meat or any other source of protein . Canda(Cow skin) is very popular
  4. Add magi and salt and then finally add oil.
  5. allow it to cook to desired texture and voila!! our Eru is ready
