Kati Kati

Description

Kati kati is a common name given to grilled chicken. It is a tradition of eating grilled chicken in meeting houses that has been popularised. It is often eaten with fufu and Njama njama

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Oil
  • Corn fufu
  • Huckle berry
  • Maggi and salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. The chicken is killed and burned till all hairs are completelyburnt
  2. It is then operated and further burned till the fire cooks it to maleability
  3. Oil is then put in a pot and the chicken further fried and maggi as well as salt and pepper added
  4. The chicken is the ready for consumption.
  5. In the cases when it is to be eaten with fufu, the fufu is prepared folloeing the fufu preparation guidelines
  6. it can also be eaten with Njama njama following its preparation guidelines
